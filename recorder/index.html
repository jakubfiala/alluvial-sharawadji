SOUNDWALK:

My Sounds

Instructions: While you explore Eufonic Festival and the Ebre Delta, use this application to record the sounds you hear along the way. They’ll be added to our interactive visualisation, along with other people’s contributions, in order to create a permanent aural record of the festival online. Listen out for ambient sounds, or add a performance of some music or a contribution of a piece of speech. Please add sounds in the position where you heard them though!

By uploading sounds, you agree to make them available under a Creative Commons CC BY-SA 4.0 licence.

Alluvial Sharawadji is a crowdsourced interactive artwork/workshop combining collaborative field recording with low-cost, ubiquitous web technologies to communicate the experience of a local sound environment to audiences who are distant in both time and space.

By Tim Cowlishaw and Jakub Fiala.
